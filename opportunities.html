Internship Experiences

Current and former members of the Collective have worked on a range of diverse projects during their studies. The features that follow are a collection of internship experiences our members have completed for real-life esteemed clients.

BCW Global Copywriter

Working on behalf of MESH Consultancy, I landed the opportunity to intern for BCW - Global as a copywriter. The client, The Rottnest Channel Swim Association (RSCA), sought a copywriter for their annual yearbook. In this role I researched and interviewed four swimmers and created four pieces of content for their 2021 Yearbook, as well as for their website and social media.

Career paths

What you can accomplish by studying communications

Digital Marketing Strategist

A Digital Marketing Strategist is pivotal in enhancing a company’s online visibility. They plan and execute marketing campaigns to promote the company’s offerings online. Using web technologies, they aim for business growth, identify new opportunities, and work towards boosting brand awareness. They also analyze web traffic metrics and suggest solutions to improve the company’s web presence.

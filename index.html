about the collective

“creating community within communications”

The Communications Collective at Murdoch University is a vibrant community where communication students connect, collaborate, and cultivate their skills.

We foster a sense of belonging and camaraderie, serving as a hub where ideas flourish, friendships form, and opportunities abound. Whether you're passionate about journalism, public relations, digital media, or any other communication field, you'll find like-minded individuals who share your enthusiasm and aspirations.

our opportunities

“connecting with professionals”

Joining the Communications Collective at Murdoch University offers a platform to connect with peers, engage with industry professionals, and access professional development.
